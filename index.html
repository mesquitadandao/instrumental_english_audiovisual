Instrumental English Audiovisual

A vocabulary english free for your language.

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

For you who need to read technically texts in the english language, perfecting your english in pronunciation and translation, but do not want to pay vocabularies or have effort to search between several words. The Instrumental English Audiovisual is a vocabulary english free for your language. Unlike other with several words to search, it only generates the translation and pronunciation of single words of the text that you will read.
